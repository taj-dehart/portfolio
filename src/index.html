    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon was an online viral campaign modeled on catholic indulgences. Users could purchase a physical or digital "pardon" for the greenhouse emissions of their friends and family. Proceeds went to hand-picked reforestation projects.
