    My work

    Mad Courses

    MAD Courses

    MAD Courses is a social business, founded to educate people around the world about indigenous wisdom and environmental stewardship. They offer unique interactive online courses, made in collaboration with local tribes in the Philippines.

    I designed MAD's website, as well as a style guide and multipurpose illustrations for their online presence.

    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon is a tongue-in-cheek awareness campaign. Inspired by catholic indulgences, the website offers physical or digital "pardons" which absolve users of their greenhouse emission sins. Proceeds go to hand-picked reforestation projects.

    Days of Type
