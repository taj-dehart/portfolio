    MAD Courses

    MAD Courses is a social business, founded to educate people around the world about indigenous wisdom and environmental stewardship. They offer unique interactive online courses, made in collaboration with local tribes in the Philippines.

    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon

    Carbon Pardon is a tongue-in-cheek awareness campaign I collaborated on. Inspired by catholic indulgences, the website offers physical or digital "pardons" which absolve users of their greenhouse emission sins. Proceeds go to hand-picked reforestation projects.
